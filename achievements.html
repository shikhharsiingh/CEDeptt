Conference Organised

Department of Computer Engineering organized 1st International Conference on Machine Learning, Image Processing, Network Security and Data sciences (MIND-2019) during 3 - 4 March, 2019. The event was technically cosponsored by IEEE Uttar Pradesh Section and technical collaborated with Springer. The conference consisted of a number of activities e.g. pre-conference workshops, technical sessions, invited talk and PhD symposium. The conference received 403 paper, out of which 97 manuscripts were selected for publication in conference proceedings. About 130 national and international delegates participated in different activities of the conference.
